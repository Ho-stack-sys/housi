LOCATED ON OLIFANTSFONTEIN ROAD, MIDRAND. THERE'S A NEW ELEPHANT
IN THE ROOM!

Welcome to Ndlovu Lifestyle Apartments; where your lights are always on, and load-shedding is a thing of the past. This secure residential estate consists of 440 rental apartments. Just like a majestic herd of African elephants, Ndlovu Lifestyle Apartments promotes a strong sense of community. Conveniently located on Olifantsfontein Road, in Midrand, it is close to several schools, shopping centres and office nodes. A singular gatehouse allows for tight security measures, to safeguard every resident and family. The property overlooks natural grasslands with walking trails ribboning past sport fields, braai areas and children’s play parks. A central clubhouse features a large swimming pool, splash zone, children’s play area, a double-storey gym, communal lounge, meeting room and laundry area. This is the ideal environment to establish your roots…
